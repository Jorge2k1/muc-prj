About us

The New School offers a vibrant academic environment, fostering innovation and creativity. Located in the heart of New York City, it combines rigorous academics with a commitment to social justice and sustainability. The university features distinguished faculty and offers a wide range of programs in design, social sciences, liberal arts, management, and the performing arts. It emphasizes hands-on learning and critical thinking, preparing students to make meaningful contributions to society.

Stunning surroundings

Situated in the vibrant and dynamic environment of New York City, The New School offers students an unparalleled urban experience. The city's cultural diversity, artistic heritage, and intellectual atmosphere provide a rich backdrop for academic and personal growth. Students have access to numerous museums, galleries, theaters, and other cultural institutions, enhancing their educational experience. The New School's location in Greenwich Village further adds to its appeal, offering a unique blend of history and modernity.
